Types of Scams

Common Scams in the Digital World

Learn about various types of scams that are prevalent today:

  • Phishing: Attempts to steal sensitive information through deceptive emails or websites.
  • Ransomware: Malicious software that locks or encrypts data until a ransom is paid.
  • Online Fraud: Scams involving fake websites or offers to trick users into providing money or personal information.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulative tactics to gain confidential information by exploiting human psychology.
